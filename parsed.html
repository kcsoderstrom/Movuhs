How dat movie rank doe?

movie rankings calculator

                          Retrieved 9 December 2014

                          I don't like to watch movies very much. I enjoy watching some movies, especially good movies, but I don't get enough out of exploring the space of interesting-sounding movies to make guess and check worth my time.

                          Crowdsourcing helps. But often I find a synopsis I like accompanied by an iffy star count. I always wonder, what does this star count mean? How much better is 7 stars than 6 stars? Is 5.5 stars actually the middle, or is that worse than average?

                          IMDB doesn't have an API. They do have a list of "all movies", paginated with 100 movies per page. (I am confident this does not comprise literally all of the movies in the database from 1970-2014, since it cuts off at a round number. I would love more information on this and where I can find an actual list of all movies; you can e-mail me here.)

                          To address this, I wrote a loop to send an ajax request to each page, then parsed each html file. I stored one average rating per movie, except for movies that had no ratings, which I discarded. (I later discovered that IMDB has some text databases; I may use those in the future.)
